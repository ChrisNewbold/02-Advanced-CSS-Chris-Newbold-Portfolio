About Me

Newbold Creative is a visual design agency with over 9 years of experience, specialising in wine, food and beverage branding and packaging design. We believe in creating the best designs to cater to your business's personality that will help establish your brand recognition. Newbold Creative bases its work on a new way of thinking within the branding and packaging design industry. We offer an honest, intuitive approach when working with our clients, and as a result, our designs are fresh, modern and exciting. The needs of our clients are our priority, and we work closely with them to achieve the best possible designs.
